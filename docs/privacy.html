Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ticket Drop Timer: Race Alerts handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Ticket Drop Timer: Race Alerts does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, address, or phone number.

The app may store your selected events, preferences, countdowns, alert settings, and notification choices locally on your device so that the app can function correctly.

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, the app may use Apple notification services to send you alerts related to selected race or ticket release countdowns. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Analytics and Tracking

Ticket Drop Timer: Race Alerts does not use third-party advertising tracking and does not sell your personal data.

If basic diagnostics or crash information is provided by Apple, it is used only to help improve app reliability and performance.

Third-Party Websites and Content

The app may display or link to publicly available event, race, or ticket information. These third-party websites and services are not controlled by us and may have their own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

If the app offers paid features or in-app purchases, purchases are processed securely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information stored locally in the app remains on your device unless you choose to share it or your device settings sync it through Apple services.

Children’s Privacy

Ticket Drop Timer: Race Alerts is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that the issue can be reviewed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
