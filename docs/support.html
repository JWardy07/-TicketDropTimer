Notifications

If alerts are not appearing, please check that notifications are enabled for the app in your device settings.

Event and Ticket Information

Ticket Drop Timer: Race Alerts is designed to help users monitor countdowns and race ticket release information. Event details and ticket availability may change, so always confirm important information with the official event organiser or ticket provider.
