FAQ

\

Is this an official HYROX app?
No. This app is unofficial and not affiliated with or endorsed by HYROX or any ticketing platform.

\

Does this app sell tickets?
No. The app tracks public event/ticket pages and links out to official pages.

\

How do I enable alerts?
Open an event, add it to your watchlist, and enable the alert toggles. Also allow notifications in iOS Settings.
